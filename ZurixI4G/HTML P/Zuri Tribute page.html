Mrs. Folorunsho Alakija

"Folorunsho Alakija does not give up, my husband does not give up and my children do not give up."

Mrs. Folorunsho Alakija is ranked the second most powerful woman in the world, the second richest woman in Africa and is the richest woman in Nigeria with a networth over $1 billon. Born 15th July, 1951, Folorunsho Alakija is a Billionaire businesswoman, philanthropist, and recently ordained Apostle of God of The Redeemed Christian Church of God. Her business ventures include fashion of the Rose of Sharon House of Fashion, printing venture of Digital Reality Prints Limited, real estate of DaySpring Property Development Company and oil and gas of Famfa Oil Limited.

Some of Folorunsho Alakija notable career, entrepreneural and philanthropic timelines include:

  • Folorunsho Alakija is vice chairman of Famfa Oil , a Nigerian oil exploration company with a stake in Agbami Oilfield, a prolific offshore asset and partner with Chevron and Petrobas.
  • With a secretarial degree, she started working at Sijuade Enterprises as an Executive Secretary. After which she worked for a while with First National Bank of Chicago (renamed, FinBank) now acquired by First City Monument Bank (FCMB) for 12 years.
  • Alongside studying secretarial studies, Folorunsho also studied Fashion designing in 1972 in Central school of Fashion, London afterwhich she returned to Nigeria to start her fashion design comany, Supreme Stiches, which was later remaned Rose of Sharon House Fashion in 1996.
  • In 1993, she gained the Oil Prospecting License(OPL) on a 617,000 acre oil block in Agbami field of the central Niger Delta.
  • Folorunsho Alakija fought the Federal Government of Nigeria in court for 12years for snatching 50% stakes from OPL 216 (her oil block). She kept fighting till she won. In her words:
    “We felt like it was unfair. We had taken the sole risk and invested everything we had in the business. It had become a family business. We spent six years as a family to ensure this worked out and now that it was bearing fruit. They just stepped in and took away everything we had struggled and worked extremely hard for. I said to myself, ‘Folorunsho Alakija does not give up, my husband does not give up and my children do not give up.”
  • Apart from running multiple businesses over the years, Folorunsho Alakija is the fisrt Nigerian female chancellor, a postion she earned at the Osun State Universty in March 2016.
  • She has received over 8 Honorary Degree Awards from universities spanning across Nigeria and the United States of America(USA)
  • Mrs. Folorunsho is the founder of the vision of Rose of Sharon Foundation(RoSF), a Non-Governmental Organization(NGO) that empowers widows and orphans through scholarships and bisiness grants and loans. A foundation that has empowered over 3000 widows through vocational skills acquisition training programmes and awarded academic scholarships up to tertiary institutional level to 1,366 widows’ children and 86 orphans.
  • She is also the primary sponsor of the Agbami Medical and Engineering Scholarship Scheme which records over 1000 beneficiaries annually.
  • Folorunsho and her husband, Mr. Modupe Alakija started a house fellowship which is now a ministry; Rose of Sharon Glorious Ministry International and in 2019, she became an Apostle of Christ under the Redeemed Christian Church of God(RCGG).
  • An author, Folorunsho has written books about marriages, her christian journey and inspirational, somes of which are titled, University of Marriage, Alone with God, A cry for Widows and Orphans, His Name Is and many more.
